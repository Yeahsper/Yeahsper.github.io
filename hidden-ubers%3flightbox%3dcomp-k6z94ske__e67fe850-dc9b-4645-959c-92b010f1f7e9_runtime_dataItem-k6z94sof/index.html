Hidden Ubers
Hidden Ubers, What?!

There is hidden evil located all throughout Sanctuary, laying in wait for the next lonely soul to wander to their lair.

Hidden Ubers are powerful bosses spread out through all Acts on Hell difficulty. 

You will want to be geared pretty well before attempting any of them.

There are currently 13 hidden ubers, with more being in development currently.

Note* There was a new Uber added in Patch 0.6.1.6. 

Each of them are guaranteed to drop either a Key of Terror, Key of Hate, or Key of Destruction.

If you can handle them, this makes them a better option compared to key farming The Countess, The Summoner, or Nithalak, which only have a 15% chance to drop a key on Hell difficulty.

There is an Exalted Chest in each Uber Zone, even on Normal, and Nightmare as well!

So with that note, lets get started.

Hidden Uber Trinket Drop Rate is 25% Chance, except for Brutalus (Furnace of Hell Act 4) which is only a 10% Drop Chance.

Enrage Information

1. Threshold (50% hp):

  • + 10% Damage Reduction

  • + 10% Total Speed

  • + 25% Total Damage 

  • + 30% Crushing Blow Resist

2. Threshold (20% hp):

  • + 20% Damage Reduction

  • + 10% Total Speed

  • + 50% Total Damage 

  • + 200% Crushing Blow Resist

 

Both Enrage effects stack.
