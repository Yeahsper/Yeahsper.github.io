Mercenaries
Basic Overview

Mercenaries ARE in Reign of Terror.  I will go over a few key points that get asked a lot.  

  • Mercenaries are Augments.

  • They can only be Augmented on your Medal

  • They can not be equipped with weapons or armor, but their stats scale with your character level so they do get stronger as you level up

  • You can not remove a Mercenary from your Medal and keep the Mercenary (You can keep the medal tho)

  • If you want to swap or switch medals, you need to buy a new Mercenary.

  • To Augment your medal with a Mercenary, Right-click the Mercenary and left-click it onto your medal.

  • After your medal has been Augmented with your Mercenary, you need to put your Mercenary on your skill bar just like a regular skill.

Mercenaries can be bought from vendors in each Act town.

  • Act 1 - Kashya

  • Act 2 - Greiz

  • Act 3 - Asheara

  • Act 4 - Grand Marshal Aurora

  • Act 5 -  Qual-Kehk

Note* You may have to talk to these people more then once to get the "Hire Mercenary" option.  So do not think they don't offer them if you don't get the option on the first talk.
